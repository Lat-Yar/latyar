About

Our Story

Jewellery can be understood as a personal status symbol, for its material properties, its patterns, or for meaningful symbols. People are demanding a jewellery that is trendy, locally handmade, high quality and finally suitable for any occasion.

LatYar (လက်ရာ) is a small handmade jewellery business from Kalaw. LatYar means "handcraft" in Myanmar language, the intention is to value the art of Pantain (gold or silver smitting) which is more than 1,200 year old. We are so proud to say that all of our products are made with pure silver that can be worn with no skin problems and delicately hand crafted.

LatYar was created by 2 founders to support local jewellery making business and donate the profits to wherever it is needed in Myanmar at this difficult time. We believe handmade goods have an expensive, soulful quality that can't be replicated any other ways.

Our Mission

Silver is durable, gorgeous, healthier, affordable, lightweight for wearing on daily routines and easy to maintain. It is budget friendly allowing people from all walks of life to own a jewellery piece. Since silver is neutral in nature, it is also more customizable with various metals and gemstones to make it more appealing and elegant.

Silver by itself can become tarnished from being exposed to the air. Although sterling silver is more durable than fine silver, the additional metals in the alloy make it more prone to tarnishing, and could even cause nickel allergic reactions to your skin.

"LatYar jewellery", only using "FINE" silver, has actual silver content of 99.9% pure silver, which is relatively soft. Most people claim that fine silver is hard to shape because of its softness unless skillful craftsmanship is the only answer.
